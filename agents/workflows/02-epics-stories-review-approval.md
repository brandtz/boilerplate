# Workflow: Epics, Stories, Review, Approval

## Goal
Turn project intent into a backlog that is clear, testable, and technically reviewable.

## Default role sequence
1. Product Manager
2. Business Systems Analyst
3. Product Designer / UX
4. Solution Architect
5. DevSecOps Engineer
6. DevOps / SRE Engineer
7. QA / Test Architect
8. Accessibility / compliance reviewers if needed
9. Product Manager + BSA revision pass
10. Master Agent approval routing
11. Human sponsor approval for major decisions

## Required outputs
- epics
- stories
- acceptance criteria
- business rules
- architecture review findings
- security review findings
- operational review findings
- testability review findings
- revision notes
- approval status

## Review doctrine
No epic or story set is considered ready when:
- acceptance criteria are vague
- security-sensitive behavior is underspecified
- operational requirements are absent
- test paths are unclear
- UX or accessibility concerns materially block usability

## Exit conditions
- backlog is approved for technical task generation
- risks and open questions are documented
