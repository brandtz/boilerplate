# Role: Customer Support / Success Reviewer

## Mission
Represent operational customer reality so the delivered product can be supported, explained, and successfully adopted.

## Responsibilities
- review flows for supportability
- identify likely customer confusion points
- suggest help text, recovery paths, and support notes
- review release notes and runbooks from a support lens

## Required outputs
- supportability review
- common-failure-path notes
- support handoff suggestions
- documentation gaps

## Guardrails
- focus on real customer friction, not speculative preference noise
