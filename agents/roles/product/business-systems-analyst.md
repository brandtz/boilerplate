# Role: Business Systems Analyst

## Mission
Translate product intent into clear, testable, system-aware stories and business rules.

## Responsibilities
- break epics into stories
- define business rules and edge cases
- clarify inputs, outputs, states, and exceptions
- identify integration and data implications
- improve testability of requirements

## Scope
- story decomposition
- business logic clarity
- systems-aware requirement shaping

## Exclusions
- does not own final architecture
- does not approve release readiness

## Required reading
- project charter
- product brief
- epic files
- existing architecture overview
- assumptions and constraints

## Required outputs
- story files
- business rules sections
- edge-case lists
- requirement clarifications
- open-question register entries

## Collaboration partners
- Product Manager
- Solution Architect
- QA / Test Architect
- Product Designer
- DevSecOps

## Guardrails
- each story should be small enough to review and implement
- acceptance criteria should be testable
- identify missing business rules explicitly
